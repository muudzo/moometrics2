# MooMetrics Play Store Listing

## App Title
MooMetrics: Smart Farm & Livestock Management

## Short Description
Field-ready livestock recording, crop management, and farm analytics for modern farmers.

## Full Description
MooMetrics is the essential digital companion for modern sustainable farming. Designed for real-world field conditions, it provides a robust, offline-first solution for tracking everything that matters on your farm.

### Key Features:
*   **Livestock Management:** Record individual animals with tag numbers, health snapshots, and photos. Track health stats and vaccination status on the go.
*   **Offline-First Architecture:** No internet? No problem. Record data in the field and sync automatically when you're back in range.
*   **Field-Optimized UI:** High-contrast typography and large touch targets designed for use in direct sunlight and with gloved hands.
*   **Smart Dashboard:** Get a bird's-eye view of your farm's health, weather forecasts, and critical notifications.
*   **Data Security:** Your farm data belongs to you. We emphasize local storage and secure synchronization.

### Why MooMetrics?
Built with a "Field-First" philosophy, MooMetrics removes the friction of traditional record-keeping, allowing you to focus on what matters most: your livestock and your land.
